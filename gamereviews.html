Videogame Reviews!

I've played a lot of video games, and I'm basically addicted to my Nintendo Switch. Here are some of my favorites and how I rate each of them! My rating system assigns 2 points each across 5 categories (story, gameplay, characters, music, and graphics) for a max score of 10. I'll sometimes allow a single category score to go higher than 2 if I think it makes up for a game's shortcomings elsewhere. I recommend anything above a 6.
