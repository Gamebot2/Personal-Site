Game Highlight

I've never been this excited for a game.
Xenoblade Chronicles 3 releases in September 2022, and it's the direct successor to the stories of both Xenoblade 1 and 2. The trailer, shown in the February 9th direct, already looks amazing. The protagonists, Noah and Mio, are from the rival nations of Keves and Agnus, and they are joined by their allies Lanz, Eunie, Sena, and Taion in a "grand adventure with 'life' as its central theme". There's a plethora of references to the previous titles in the trailer, including the Mechonis Sword and Urayan Titan in the background of the image above. I cannot wait to get my hands on this one.

I've played a lot of video games, and I'm basically addicted to my Nintendo Switch. Here are some of my favorites and how I rate each of them! My rating system assigns 2 points each across 5 categories (story, gameplay, characters, music, and graphics) for a max score of 10. I'll sometimes allow a single category score to go higher than 2 if I think it makes up for a game's shortcomings elsewhere. I recommend anything above a 6.
